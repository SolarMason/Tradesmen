The Legal Architecture

Three pillars. Zero overlap. Iron-clad.

NEPA-PRO Tradesmen is structured deliberately: NEPA-PRO LLC operates the labor staffing platform; every tradesperson is a vetted independent 1099 subcontractor who has signed a six-document onboarding packet; and every customer accepts a three-document booking-protection packet at checkout. Each document does one job — that's how we keep the model rock-solid without contradictions.

1

Platform & Vetting

NEPA-PRO LLC is a Pennsylvania platform that vets, dispatches, and processes payment. Every dispatched tradesperson is a verified independent contractor with active license, insurance, and background check on file before their first job.

2

Subcontractor Packet

Six documents establish the 1099 relationship: master agreement, tax info, FCRA-compliant background authorization, banking, credentials & insurance proof, and field-conduct standards. No tradesperson is dispatched without the packet countersigned.

3

Customer Protections

Three documents cover the customer side: master service terms, jobsite safety attestation (with hazardous-material disclosure and PA One Call utility marking), and a liability waiver capping risk per booking. Accepted at Stripe checkout; signed copies available on request.

Worker Onboarding · 1099 Subcontractor Packet

Six documents. Every subcontractor signs all six.

Before any job dispatch, every tradesperson on the NEPA-PRO Tradesmen platform completes the full onboarding packet. Each document covers a single, distinct legal or operational purpose — there is no overlap, no redundancy, and no tradesperson skips a document.

Customer Documents · Booking Protections

Three documents. Accepted at checkout, signed on request.

Customers accept these three documents by submitting payment through Stripe checkout — they're incorporated by reference into every booking. Wet-signature copies are available for general contractors, property managers, and commercial customers who maintain a paper file for every engagement.

NPCS-001 · 5 pages · v1.0

Customer Service Agreement & Terms

Master booking terms: service description, what's included and what's excluded (engineering, permits, materials, hazmat remediation), payment, overtime, cancellation tiers, customer obligations, AS-IS labor disclaimer, 30-day workmanship warranty, and Pennsylvania governing law.

↓ Download PDF View
NPCS-002 · 4 pages · v1.0

Jobsite Safety Attestation

Customer's pre-dispatch certification: PA One Call (811) utility marking, hazardous-material disclosure (asbestos, lead, mold, PCBs, oil tanks), site access, lighting, on-site contact, and acknowledgment of the tradesperson's stop-work authority for unsafe conditions.

↓ Download PDF View
NPCS-003 · 4 pages · v1.0

Liability Waiver & Indemnification

Allocation of risk: customer indemnification for undisclosed jobsite conditions; liability cap equal to the booking amount; exclusion of consequential damages; 30-day claim notice; mediation, optional binding arbitration in Scranton, jury-trial and class-action waivers.
